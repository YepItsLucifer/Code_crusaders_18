-- Basic Tips for planting --

  1. Give your plants plenty of sunlight based on their specific needs.
  2. Water your plants regularly so that the soil is moist but not wet.
  3. Fertilize your plants every few weeks so they get the required nutrients.
  4. Cut off any dead or diseased limbs to keep your plant healthy.
